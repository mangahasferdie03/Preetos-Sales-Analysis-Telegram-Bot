Y - This is the year of the order date
M - This is the month of the order date
Order Date - Date the order was placed
Name - Customer name
Sold By - Dropdown: Ferdie for Quezon City pick-ups, Nina for Paranaque

G - Payment mode dropdown (typically Gcash, BPI, Cash)
H - Payment Status (Paid/Unpaid)
I - Delivery Status

Product Quantities - Pouch varieties:
N - Cheese Pouch
O - Sour Cream Pouch
P - BBQ Pouch
Q - Original Pouch

Product Quantities - Tub varieties (2L sizes):
T - Cheese Tub
U - Sour Cream Tub
V - BBQ Tub
W - Original Tub

Financial columns:
Z - SF (Shipping fee)
AB - Disc (Discount given)
AC - Price (auto-calculated)

Metadata:
Notes column
Summary Order (auto-generated breakdown)
Several blank/deprecated columns marked for leaving empty
